About the App

One Candle Ahead is a trading simulation game that lets you practice stock and crypto trading using real historical market data. Predict the next candle and see if you can beat the market!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real trading?

No. One Candle Ahead is a simulation game. No real money is involved, and you cannot make or lose actual money.

Where does the chart data come from?

All charts are based on real historical price data from actual stock markets and cryptocurrency exchanges.

How do I reset my progress?

You can sign out and create a new account, or contact us to request a data reset.

What does Premium include?

Premium removes all ads, unlocks unlimited games (no blue chip wait), and gives you a special badge on the leaderboard.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Premium and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.
